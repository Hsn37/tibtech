Multimodal Artificial Intelligence-driven Livestock Analytics
Tibbling Technologies' multimodal AI framework for your end-to-end livestock analytics.

Asim Iqbal, PhD

Livestock farming faces increasing challenges as farms scale up, making it harder to monitor individual animals. Traditional methods for assessing animal health and behavior are labor-intensive, prone to human error, and lack real-time insights. As a result, unnoticed health issues or behavioral changes can lead to productivity losses and impact animal welfare. Farmers need a modern solution that automates these processes and provides actionable insights to improve decision-making and operational efficiency.

We address these challenges with AnimalFormer, our multimodal AI framework that delivers end-to-end livestock analytics designed for the modern farm.

Our Multimodal AI framework for end-to-end livestock analytics


AnimalFormer operates through a simple, non-invasive process that begins with video footage of your livestock. Whether it’s cows, sheep, poultry, or pigs, we use these videos as input, and our advanced AI handles the rest. Using cutting-edge computer vision and multimodal analysis, our framework delivers insights such as behavioral analytics, nutrition optimization, health monitoring, and production metrics.




Our framework provides real-time, actionable information to help farmers detect changes in animal behavior, optimize feeding strategies, track vital signs, and monitor productivity—all without intrusive methods. With our framework, you gain a comprehensive understanding of your livestock, enabling enhanced animal welfare and improved operational efficiency.





Detailed insights

Through cutting-edge segmentation and pose estimation technology, our framework tracks both individual animals and group dynamics. Whether it’s monitoring grazing patterns, detecting signs of illness through movement analysis, or assessing social behavior, our platform provides farmers with the detailed metrics they need to improve livestock welfare and operational efficiency.



Our AI-powered platform delivers a wide range of analytics tailored to the needs of today’s livestock farms. These insights enable farmers to make informed decisions, optimize feeding strategies, monitor vital signs, and improve overall productivity—all with ease and precision.








Performance comparison

The model in our framework consistently outperforms the competition, achieving up to 23% higher accuracy. This level of precision ensures more reliable data for tracking animal health, behavior, and production, helping farmers make data-driven decisions with confidence.







The future of livestock management

Our framework is the next step in the evolution of livestock farming, offering a comprehensive, AI-driven solution that enhances both productivity and animal welfare. With its precision and non-invasive approach, it empowers farmers to make informed, data-backed decisions with ease. By integrating real-time analytics and behavioral insights, our product ensures that farms remain efficient, sustainable, and responsive to the needs of their animals.
