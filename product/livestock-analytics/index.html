Multimodal Artificial Intelligence-driven Livestock Analytics
Tibbling Technologies' multimodal AI framework for your end-to-end livestock analytics.

Asim Iqbal, PhD

Livestock farming faces increasing challenges as farms scale up, and monitoring individual animals becomes more difficult. Traditional methods of assessing animal health and behavior are labor-intensive, prone to human error, and lack real-time insights. As a result, unnoticed behavioral changes or health issues can lead to significant productivity losses and affect animal welfare. Farmers need a solution that not only automates this process but also provides actionable insights to improve decision-making and operational efficiency.

We address these challenges with AnimalFormer, our multimodal AI framework that provides end-to-end livestock analytics designed for the modern farm.

Our Multimodal AI framework for end-to-end livestock analytics


AnimalFormer framework operates through a simple, non-invasive process that starts with video footage of your livestock. Whether it’s cows, sheep, poultry, or pigs, we use these videos as input, and our advanced AI handles the rest. Using cutting-edge computer vision and multimodal analysis, our framework processes this data to deliver a wide range of insights, including behavioral analytics, nutrition optimization, health monitoring, production metrics, and much more.



Our framework provides real-time, actionable information to help farmers detect changes in animal behavior, optimize feeding strategies, track vital signs, and monitor productivity, all without the need for intrusive methods. With AnimalFormer, you gain a comprehensive understanding of your livestock, enabling you to enhance animal welfare and boost operational efficiency effortlessly.





Detailed insights

Behind the scenes, our powerful AI framework processes the video footage to provide detailed insights into each animal’s movement and behavior, offering valuable information for optimizing care, monitoring health, and improving overall farm management. Whether you’re monitoring a herd or an individual animal, our solution brings real-time, actionable data to your fingertips, making it effortless to keep your livestock thriving.



Through advanced segmentation and pose estimation, our framework can identify and analyze individual animals within a group, allowing for precise tracking of both individual and collective behavior. This means that you can gain insights into the health of individual animals by monitoring their movement patterns—whether they are running, walking, or resting—as well as the group dynamics, such as grazing patterns that impact nutrition or social interactions that provide indicators of overall behavioral health. By observing these detailed metrics, farmers can make informed decisions on feeding strategies, health interventions, and management of livestock well-being, ensuring each animal receives the care it needs for optimal productivity.





Performance comparison

The models in our multimodal AI framework for livestock analytics surpass other models in terms of accuracy and performance. As shown in the comparison, our system achieves significantly better results—up to 23% higher precision compared to competing models. This means more reliable and actionable insights for farmers, ensuring that every aspect of livestock behavior, health, and production is monitored with unparalleled accuracy. Whether it’s tracking animal movements or assessing wellness, our system provides a level of precision that sets a new standard in livestock analytics, helping farmers make data-driven decisions with confidence.






The future of livestock management

Our framework is the next step in the evolution of livestock farming, offering a comprehensive, AI-driven solution that enhances both productivity and animal welfare. With its precision and non-invasive approach, it empowers farmers to make informed, data-backed decisions with ease. By integrating real-time analytics and behavioral insights, our product ensures that farms remain efficient, sustainable, and responsive to the needs of their animals.
